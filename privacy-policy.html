Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Floppy Pixel app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Mustafa Aljaburi (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application itself does not require registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information from you. Game preferences such as high scores, sound settings, and game progress are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to the Service Provider or any third party.

However, the Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device. These third-party services include:

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The Application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising Identifier / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • General device information (device model, operating system version)

Before AdMob collects your Advertising Identifier, the Application will display Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking for your permission. If you decline, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads and will not access your Advertising Identifier. You can learn more about Google's data practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Apple Game Center

The Application integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. When you use Game Center, Apple may collect your Game Center player ID, scores, and achievement data. This data is handled by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/privacy/.

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

The Application offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. The Service Provider does not collect or have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

App Tracking Transparency

The Application uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You will be prompted to allow or deny tracking when you first use the Application. If you allow tracking, AdMob may use your Advertising Identifier to deliver personalized ads. If you deny tracking, only non-personalized (contextual) ads will be shown. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

The Application stores the following data locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • High score
  • Total coins collected
  • Sound preference (on/off)
  • Ad removal purchase status
  • Game attempt count

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may also:

  • Deny tracking when prompted by the ATT dialog, or revoke it later in your device Settings
  • Purchase the "Remove Ads" option to eliminate all ad-related data collection
  • Reset your Advertising Identifier in your device Settings

Children

The Application is not used to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Service Provider encourages all children to never submit any personally identifiable information through the Application and/or Services. The Service Provider encourages parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce this Policy by instructing their children never to provide personally identifiable information through the Application and/or Services without their permission. If you have reason to believe that a child has provided personally identifiable information to the Service Provider through the Application and/or Services, please contact the Service Provider (maljaburi@gmail.com) so that they will be able to take the necessary actions. You must also be at least 16 years of age to consent to the processing of your personally identifiable information in your country (in some countries we may allow your parent or guardian to do so on your behalf).
